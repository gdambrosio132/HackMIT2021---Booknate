Mission Statement

The BOOKNATE team are bringing books to students in need around the world, and we need your help! Our goal is to bring education to students in a way never done before. Here you can donate any unused books, textbooks, materials, and anything that will help a students with little access to resources and we will do all the work in sending books to these students and bring a smile to their faces!
